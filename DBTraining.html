WHO WE ARE.

What is LSJSK?

LSJSK is an acronym that stands for Life Skills for Job Seekers-Kenya. The organization was started in 2018 to help Kenyan graduates to successfully navigate through the tough job-searching terrain in Kenya.

Our Values

Openness, honesty, integrity, courage,
respect,
diversity, and balance.

Our Mission

A prosperous society through enabling holistic, creative education unique to every individual. At Yusudi, we envision a world where every young person has an opportunity to develop and apply their best talents for self-fulfilment and common wealth.
